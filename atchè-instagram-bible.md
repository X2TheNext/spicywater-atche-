## VOICE RULES — MONET'S VOICE

✓ First person. Always "I" and "we" — never "ATCHÉ believes."  
✓ Specific. She names real moments, real feelings, real reasons.  
✓ Direct. She addresses the woman reading. "You." Not "women like you."  
✓ End with one clear ask. Never two.  
✗ No exclamation marks.  
✗ No "we're excited to announce."  
✗ No corporate language.  
✗ No emojis in caption body.  
✗ Xavier's name or perspective never appears in captions.  
✗ The account never sounds like it's being run by someone else.
