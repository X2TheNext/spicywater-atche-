03

Code of Conduct

The standard.
Non-negotiable.

ATCHÉ's reputation is built on quality and professionalism. Every Circle member is a direct reflection of the brand. These standards apply before, during, and after every engagement.

Before the Shoot

  • Confirm your attendance when you receive your Shoot Confirmation. Silence is not confirmation.
  • Outfit confirmed in advance. Your look is agreed upon before the date. No surprises.
  • Arrive camera-ready. Hair, makeup, and nails are done before you walk in. We do not have a glam team on site.
  • Know the venue, know the time. Plan to arrive 20–30 minutes before your confirmed call time.

During the Shoot

  • Sober and present. You may enjoy the venue's hospitality — you are not there to get drunk. Impairment during a production is an immediate removal from the session and a formal warning.
  • Take direction. The creative director's call is final on set. Questions are welcome; arguments are not.
  • Phone on silent and away unless you're creating content. Scrolling during production is unprofessional.
  • Treat venue staff with respect. They are our clients' team. How you treat them is how we treat our clients.
  • Treat every other Circle member with respect. No exceptions. No drama on set.

After the Shoot

  • Post within 24 hours of receiving your content. Tag @atcheofficial and the venue handle in every post. This is part of your agreement — not optional.
  • Keep posts live for 90 days minimum. Do not archive or delete without approval.
  • Credit ATCHÉ in your caption — in the text, not just as a tag.
  • No disparaging content about the venue, clients, or other Circle members — publicly or privately in ways that could become public.

At All Times

  • Confidentiality. Client names, rates, other members' personal information, and ATCHÉ's business operations are private. Sharing them is a breach and grounds for removal.
  • No direct solicitation. Do not contact ATCHÉ venues or clients directly to offer your services independently. This violates your agreement and damages the brand that is building your career.
  • Your social media reflects on ATCHÉ. This does not mean we police your personal expression. It means use judgment. Publicly inflammatory, violent, or discriminatory content is incompatible with Circle membership.

Timeliness Policy

On time means
early.

We shoot on a schedule for paying venue clients. Every minute we wait costs the production. Consistent lateness is not tolerated and will not be excused.

The standard: Arrive 20–30 minutes before your confirmed call time. If your call time is 7:00 PM, you should be walking in at 6:30–6:40 PM. That is on time for ATCHÉ.

Situation Consequence Status
On time or early No action. This is the standard. Good Standing
1–15 min late Verbal note on file. Third occurrence = written warning. Noted
15–30 min late Written warning. Session fee reduced by 25% for that session. Warning 1
30+ min late Removed from session. No compensation. Formal written warning. Warning 2
Second formal warning 30-day suspension from Circle bookings. Suspended
Third formal warning Permanent removal from the Circle. Removed

Traffic, transit, life

We understand DC traffic is real. If something is genuinely out of your control, communicate with us before your call time — not when you're already late. Members who communicate proactively are treated differently than members who go silent and show up 40 minutes behind.

05

Cancellation & No-Show Policy

If you can't make it,
say so. Fast.

Life happens. We respect that. What we do not tolerate is silence, last-minute bail, or ghost behavior. When you cancel without notice, we have to scramble to find a replacement and that reflects on every woman in the Circle, not just you.

Notice Given What Happens Status
72+ hours before No penalty. Spot opened to waitlist. Thank you for the heads up. No Issue
48–72 hours before No penalty first time. Second occurrence = written warning. Grace
24–48 hours before Written warning. Loss of priority booking for next two sessions. Warning 1
Under 24 hours Written warning. 2-week suspension from bookings. No compensation if session already began. Warning 2
No-show (no contact) Immediate removal from the Circle roster. No exceptions without documented emergency. Removed

The No-Show Rule

A no-show with no communication is the single fastest way to lose your Circle membership permanently. We are operating inside client venues with schedules and expectations. Ghosting a shoot is not just a personal decision — it damages ATCHÉ's relationship with that client and makes it harder for every other woman in the Circle to work. We take it seriously.

Emergency Exceptions

Genuine emergencies — medical, family, safety — are handled with compassion on a case-by-case basis. Document what happened and communicate with us as soon as you're able. We are not unreasonable. But "I forgot" and "something came up" are not emergencies.

How to Cancel

  • Message @atcheofficial on Instagram or email thedigitalmarxman@gmail.com
  • Include your name, the shoot date, venue, and reason
  • Wait for confirmation that your cancellation was received
  • Silence after sending is not confirmation — follow up if you don't hear back within 2 hours

Content & Posting

Your post is
part of the deal.

The content you post after each shoot is a core deliverable — not a courtesy. When you joined the Circle you agreed to post. This is how ATCHÉ generates organic reach for our venue clients and how your value to the brand grows over time.

  • Post within 24 hours of receiving your content from ATCHÉ.
  • Tag @atcheofficial in the caption text and as an Instagram tag — both.
  • Tag the venue handle as specified in your Shoot Confirmation.
  • Keep posts live for 90 days minimum. Archiving counts as removal.
  • Stories are encouraged but do not replace feed posts. We need the permanence of a feed post.
  • Caption the photo meaningfully. "Had a great night!" is not the move. Write something real about the experience.
  • Do not add competing brand tags to posts featuring ATCHÉ content without prior approval.

Failure to Post

Failure to post after receiving content is a breach of your member agreement. First occurrence: written warning and loss of next session's priority booking. Second occurrence: suspension. We do not compensate members who fail to post — the content and the post are part of one transaction.

Before Every Shoot

Submit your
wardrobe.

ATCHÉ shoots are editorial — not club nights. Your look must be approved 48 hours before every shoot. This is non-negotiable and protects everyone's time.

  • Submit 2–3 outfit options to Monet via DM or text — full body photo, shoes included, 48 hours before the shoot.
  • Wait for written confirmation before the shoot. You will receive a confirmed look or specific notes within 24 hours.
  • Arriving in an unapproved look may result in your spot being held for another member. We run tight productions.
  • The standard: DC Friday night done right — sexy and intentional. Freakum dress energy is welcome. Ill-fitting, mismatched, or overly revealing outfits are not. When in doubt, review the Style Guide.

08

Compensation

How you
get paid.

Your rate is confirmed in your Shoot Confirmation before every session. ATCHÉ collects agency fees from venue clients and pays you directly within 48 hours of shoot completion. You never invoice a client or chase payment.

  • Rate confirmation happens in writing before you agree to any shoot. You will always know your number before you say yes.
  • Payment method is Cash App, Zelle, or platform transfer — confirmed in your Shoot Confirmation.
  • Payment timeline is within 48 hours of shoot completion, contingent on posting requirements being met.
  • ATCHÉ negotiates on your behalf. You do not set rates with clients directly. All pricing goes through ATCHÉ. This protects your leverage.
  • If you cancel under 24 hours and the session proceeds without you, no compensation is owed.
  • If ATCHÉ cancels the session, you receive a kill fee of 25% of your confirmed rate.
  • Rates increase as the brand grows and as you build your track record. Higher-paying brand events and activations are offered to members in good standing first.
  • Tax responsibility is yours as an independent contractor. ATCHÉ will issue a 1099 if you earn over $600 in a calendar year.

Growing Your Rate

Members who consistently show up on time, deliver excellent content, and maintain their professional obligations are prioritized for higher-paying engagements — brand events, private parties, corporate buyouts. The fastest path to earning more is being someone we can always count on.

Warning System

Three strikes.
You're out.

Every formal warning is documented and communicated to you in writing. You will always know where you stand.

Warning LevelResult
Warning 1 Written notice. Documented in your member file. No immediate booking loss.
Warning 2 Written notice. 30-day suspension from Circle bookings. No compensation during suspension.
Warning 3 Permanent removal from the Circle. Member agreement terminated.
Immediate removal No-show without contact. Impairment on set. Breach of confidentiality. Direct solicitation of ATCHÉ clients. Any conduct that damages the brand.

Removal from the Circle does not affect your right to keep content already delivered to you. It does terminate all future bookings, priority access, and compensation.
